Shuttleasy
	This is my group graduation project and it's not over yet(there may be some confusion).I am the .net backend developer of this project and I am using Azure cloud system for server.
	If I had to summarize project,From students to factory workers, there are plenty of people from different age groups for whom it is a necessity to use shuttle buses in their daily lives. From their perspective, it is very vital for them to get in and get out of their shuttles on time in order to catch up their daily responsibilities. We all are aware that time is one of the most important things in our lives. Nobody wants to wait for their service shuttle for a long time, or worse, to be forgotten by the shuttle driver. Moreover, we can’t ignore the fact that it is always better to know your shuttle’s arrival time in order to schedule your day and use your time more efficiently. Next, in such conditions that you entrust your life to somebody else, traceability can be vital. Thus, it is very important for shuttle companies to meet all concerns of passengers and their loved ones. People who need a shuttle want to find a shuttle company suitable and flexible for their needs. Yet, they still usually stay with traditional options. When there is a situation such as being late for a couple minutes, they need to get in touch with the driver using old school methods. With this application, basic communication will be easy as pie. Don’t you know which shuttle company to travel with? In case there are multiple options, it will be easy for you to choose the best shuttle company from different alternatives in this application.
The most important aims of our project are:
	1. To allow passengers to track their shuttles on the online map and be able to rearrange their upcoming journeys,
	2. To provide an optimal route for drivers in order to pick up all passengers in a time-efficient way,
	3. To provide managers of the shuttle companies to track their drivers, busses state (if buses are defected or not) and adding/editing/removing passengers, drivers, buses, new shuttle sessions etc.
